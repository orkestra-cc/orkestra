# Stripe & Payments

The `payments` module wraps the Stripe gateway. The `subscriptions` module sits on top of it, providing the catalog of services Tier-2 clients can subscribe to.

## Why these two modules matter architecturally

`payments` and `subscriptions` are **not ordinary feature addons** — they're the mechanism by which Tier-2 (external client) tenants consume Tier-1-hosted services. See [Two-tier tenancy](/architecture/tenancy-model).
